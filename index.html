How It Works

  • This system uses genetic algorithms to evolve digital artworks
  • Each artwork has a "genome" that defines its colors, shapes, and movements
  • Switch between different fitness environments to evolve different art styles
  • Use multi-objective optimization to balance different aesthetic criteria
  • Click the ★ star to save artworks to your favorites
  • Breeding Mode: Click two favorites to cross-breed them directly
  • Genome Comparison: Compare any two genomes visually and statistically
  • Export Hall of Fame: Download all top genomes as a ZIP file
  • Use preset combos for quick environment + mutation combinations
  • Track evolution progress with the fitness history chart
